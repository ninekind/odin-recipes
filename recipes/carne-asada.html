Carne Asada Recipe!

Description

This is the recipe for delicious tender carne asada, perfect for tacos!!

Ingredients

  • Citrus Juices for tenderizing
  • Soy sauce
  • Seasoning and Spices
  • Chipotle peppers or peppers of choice
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cut limes for a tenderizing marinade
  2. Optional to add mexican beer of choice
  3. Add fave seasonings
  4. Let sit in fridge for 8hrs
